PATENT ANALYSIS – GRACE AI PHONE ASSISTANT

(Draft for internal documentation and Claude Code reference)

1. Executive Summary

Grace is an AI-powered trade service communication assistant designed specifically for contractor businesses such as electrical, plumbing, HVAC, pond maintenance, and similar field service industries.

The system performs functions including:

Answering business calls using an AI voice agent

Collecting structured job information from clients

Automatically generating structured summaries

Sending SMS summaries to the business owner

Receiving client-submitted photos via MMS

Forwarding photos and job summaries to the contractor

Filtering telemarketers and spam calls

Integrating with estimating, scheduling, and job management systems

Unlike general AI voice assistants, Grace is purpose-built for field service contractors and acts as a job intake and triage system rather than a general conversational assistant.

The potential patentable novelty lies in the combination of voice intake, structured job capture, media processing, and contractor workflow automation.

However, most focus on:

General AI Voice Assistants

Examples include systems by:

Amazon (Alexa voice interaction patents)

Google (voice assistant conversation processing)

Apple (Siri voice interaction patents)

These patents primarily address:

speech recognition

natural language processing

conversational AI systems

They do not focus specifically on contractor job intake workflows.

Call Routing and Voice IVR Systems

Companies with patents here include:

Cisco

Twilio

Genesys

These systems cover:

automated call routing

IVR systems

AI call agents

call center automation

However, these systems do not integrate job estimation workflows or contractor-specific intake logic.

Field Service Software Platforms

Companies like:

ServiceTitan

Jobber

Housecall Pro

focus on:

scheduling

dispatching

job management

invoicing

These systems typically require manual job entry and do not include AI voice job intake with multimedia forwarding.

AI Call Agents

Platforms like:

Retell AI

conversational AI

call automation

4. Potentially Patentable Components of Grace

The following components may contain novel combinations that could be patentable.

4.1 AI Contractor Job Intake System

A system where an AI voice agent:

Answers a contractor's business phone

Identifies the caller as a potential client

Conducts a structured intake process

Collects information such as

customer name

phone number

address

job description

urgency level

trade-specific details

Converts this into a structured job ticket automatically

Potential Patent Claim Concept

A system and method for automated job intake for field service contractors using conversational AI.

4.2 AI Call Summary + MMS Media Forwarding

One of the strongest novelty areas in Grace.

Workflow:

Customer sends photos via SMS/MMS

AI system confirms receipt

Images are stored temporarily

Images are attached to the call summary

Summary + photos are sent to the contractor

Key novelty

The combination of call summary generation + MMS media forwarding in contractor workflows.

Potential claim:

A communication system that aggregates voice intake information and multimedia attachments into a structured job summary for transmission to a service provider.

4.3 Telemarketer Filtering for Contractor Phone Systems

Grace can identify:

spam callers

telemarketers

robocalls

Then automatically:

terminate the call

prevent summary generation

log the event

Potential claim:

A conversational AI system configured to identify and filter non-service-related callers from contractor business phone lines.

4.4 Trade-Specific AI Question Trees

Grace asks different questions depending on the trade.

Examples:

Electrical:

breaker tripping?

panel type?

new circuit needed?

Pond maintenance:

pump failure?

algae buildup?

fountain issues?

Potential claim:

An adaptive AI intake system configured to modify job intake questions based on the trade classification of a service business.

5. Patent Risk Assessment

The biggest risk areas are:

Voice AI Technology

Speech recognition and conversation engines are heavily patented.

Grace should not claim invention of voice AI itself, but instead focus on:

contractor workflow automation

job intake processes

multimedia summary generation

SMS Messaging Systems

Messaging protocols are widely patented.

However, workflow-based automation involving job summaries + media could be novel.

6. Strongest Patent Opportunity

The most defensible concept is likely:

AI Contractor Intake and Job Triage System

Possible title:

"AI-Driven Contractor Job Intake and Multimedia Work Order Generation System"

Key components:

AI voice call intake

Structured job capture

SMS photo capture

Automatic call summary generation

Contractor notification workflow
